Preface

WordMath is an add-in for Word that allows you to perform a wide range of mathematical operations directly from Word. It is targeted primary and secondary education, but can easily be applied to higher education.

WordMath core is free and released under the GNU General Public License.

 

It is possible to purchase a partnership that provides access to WordMath+, support and a number of additional services that make the work of getting WordMath running on many computers much easier.

At the same time, it is the partner schools that help to ensure that WordMath is continuously updated and further developed.
