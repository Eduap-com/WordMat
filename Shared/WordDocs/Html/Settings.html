Settings

You can find WordMath's settings via the menu on the left side. Here you can quickly change the most frequently used settings, or you can click on the gear to see all options. The default shortcut to open the settings is Alt + J.

The following describes some of the settings you can make in WordMath, but not all. Many of the recommendations are described in the sections where they are relevant. The tabs Graph, Units, Num. Equation & Code each have their own section further down.
